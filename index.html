Counseling and Food Insecurity Resources

Zoom Counseling Sessions: 1-800-434-3000
International Warm Line (Peer-to-Peer Counseling): 1-866-771-9276
Crisis Services: 1-888-568-1112
 
The CAPTAIN'S CUPBOARD on campus food pantry is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 am until 5:00 pm for self-service.  If you are experiencing food insecurity, please stop by and pick up what you need.
  • Use the entrance to located on McKernan Drive (the side with the ramp).
  • Cloth face coverings (masks) must be worn.
  • Social distancing rules must be maintained (six feet between users).
